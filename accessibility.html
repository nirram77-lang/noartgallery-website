Accessibility Statement

Making stories accessible to everyone

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment

No Art Gallery is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

We believe that stories should be accessible to all, regardless of ability. Our goal is to create an inclusive environment where everyone can explore and engage with our worlds.

Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following accessibility features on our website:

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • High contrast color schemes for better readability
  • Keyboard navigation support throughout the site
  • Alt text for all meaningful images
  • Consistent and clear navigation structure
  • Resizable text without loss of functionality
  • Focus indicators for interactive elements
  • ARIA labels where appropriate

Standards We Follow

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more user-friendly for everyone.

Continuous Improvement

We are constantly working to improve the accessibility of our website. Our team regularly reviews the site for accessibility issues and works to resolve them promptly.

We welcome your feedback and are committed to addressing any accessibility barriers you may encounter.

Third-Party Content

Our website may link to third-party websites and services. While we strive to link only to sites that share our commitment to accessibility, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of external content.
